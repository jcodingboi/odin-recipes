Spinach

A delicious recipe for sautéed spinach

This is a recipe for a simple and nutritious spinach dish.

Ingredients

  • Fresh spinach
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Lemon juice (optional)

Steps

  1. Wash and dry the spinach leaves
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat
  3. Add minced garlic and sauté for about 30 seconds
  4. Add spinach leaves and cook until wilted, about 2-3 minutes
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste
  6. Optional: Squeeze fresh lemon juice over the spinach
  7. Serve immediately
